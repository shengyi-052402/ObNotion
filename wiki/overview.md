# Vault Overview

This vault is organized as a persistent markdown wiki for incrementally maintained knowledge.

## Layers

- `raw/` stores source material and inbox captures.
- `wiki/` stores synthesized knowledge pages maintained over time.
- `system/` stores process and templates.

## Current State

- The vault scaffold is in place.
- No domain-specific sources have been ingested yet.
- Future work should start by placing source material in `raw/inbox/` and processing it into the wiki.

## Next Actions

- Add clipped articles, papers, transcripts, or notes to `raw/inbox/`.
- Process one source at a time into `raw/sources/` and related wiki pages.
- Keep `wiki/index.md` and `wiki/log.md` current on every meaningful update.
